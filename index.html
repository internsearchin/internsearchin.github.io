What we're doing?

Connections

Being an international community of developers, we help you to build connections with the leaders in the industry. You learn by contributing in their projects.

Teaching to code

We help you to stay consistent in coding practices by helping you learn Data Structures and Algorithms and implementing it to solve coding problems.

Development

We equip you with the basics of Development and open source contribution. Developing something makes you feel like "GOD" after you create your product.

Online Sessions

We conduct online sessions every weekend with an Industry expert where our community members get a chance to clear their doubts and decide their future plans.

About us


What's AlgoMagnet?

AlgoMagnet is a commmunity which aims towards helping you learn to code( Data Structures and Algorithm). We provide development skills, work from home projects, to make you employable.

Why to Join?

To be part of coding community to learn development skills, solve problems through technology and to get inspired to be world class developer and to contribute positively in industry and human-life.

Happy audience

Our sessions are intended for University students and any others including industry experts and faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.
