About us


What's AlgoMagnet?

AlgoMagnet is a commmunity which aims towards helping you learn to code( Data Structures and Algorithm). We provide development skills, work from home projects, to make you employable.

Why to Join?

To be part of coding community to learn development skills, solve problems through technology and to get inspired to be world class developer and to contribute positively in industry and human-life.

Happy audience

Our sessions are intended for University students and any others including industry experts and faculty members who want to learn development skills & work to solve real-life problems.
